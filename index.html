Being Trans in Philosophy: Stories

This zine is born out of our collective frustration that we as a profession have failed to reckon with the material, on-the-ground consequences of academic philosophy's role as the main discipline providing intellectual cover for a global transmisogynistic and transphobic moral panic, which is being increasingly institutionalized into laws and polices. It tells firsthand stories of how philosophy has materially dimished and endangered the lives of trans people, including our trans colleagues, students, and kids.

We encourage allies to make and distribute copies at their discretion (at conferences, in your departments, etc.).
